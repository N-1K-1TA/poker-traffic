About

We’re poker players ourselves — and we got tired of the same problem: finding a good place to play often turns into a time-wasting quest.

You register in a room or club, log in, explore the interface — and then realize there’s no real game running. No tables, no players.

That’s why we created this platform — to show real activity and help you spend less time searching and more time playing.

FAQ

Is it free? +
Yes — the platform is free to use. You can browse traffic statistics, apply filters, and explore activity without registration or payment.
How can I know if a room/club has an active game? +
Use the filters to choose where you want to play. After that, check the Statistics table — it shows how many active tables and players there are at different times of the day.

If there are no active tables, you’ll see it right away.
In what currency are the limits displayed? +
All limits on the website are displayed in USD. If a club uses another currency (for example BRL or INR), we convert the limits into USD and show them in a single standard format — the same applies to all rooms and clubs.
How up-to-date is the information on the website? +
We update and maintain the data regularly. The Statistics block shows the “Last update” time, so you can always see how fresh the information is.
If I have additional questions or suggestions, how can I contact you? +
You can reach us through the Contacts section on the website. We welcome feedback, suggestions, and partnership inquiries — your input helps us improve the platform and make it more useful for players.
